Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Grill Gurus BBQ ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Grill Gurus BBQ mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our App:

  • Name: When you post a review or share a photo in the community section, you provide your name.
  • Photos: When you choose to share a photo in the community section, we access your device's photo library with your permission.
  • Order information: When you place an order, we collect your order details (items, quantities, and prices).
  • Device notifications token: If you enable push notifications, we store a device token to send you truck location alerts and Lamb Saturday reminders.
  • Usage data: We may collect general, non-identifiable usage data such as app interactions to improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name and photos in the community section of the App.
  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To send push notifications about the food truck's live location and upcoming events.
  • To improve the App's features and performance.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Community posts (photos, reviews, and names) are visible to other users of the App. We may use third-party services (such as Expo and Firebase) to help operate the App; these services have their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. Community photos and reviews are stored on our servers. Order data and notification preferences may be stored locally on your device using secure storage.

5. Permissions We Request

  • Photo Library: To let you choose a photo to share in the community section.
  • Camera: To let you take a photo directly to share in the community section.
  • Notifications: To alert you when the Grill Gurus truck goes live and for event reminders.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data from our systems.
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
